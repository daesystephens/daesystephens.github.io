# Comparison Operators

| __Operator__       | __Description__          |
|--------------------|--------------------------|
|``` ==``` |IS EQUAL TO               |
|```!=```  |IS NOT EQUAL TO           |
|``` ===```|STRICT EQUAL TO           |
|``` !==```|STRICT NOT EQUAL TO       |
|``` >```  |GREATER THAN              |
|``` <```  |LESS THAN                 |
|``` >=``` |GREATHER THAN OR EQUAL TO |
|``` <=``` |LESS THAN OR EQUAL TO     |

# Logical Operators

**&&**
| __logic__|
|```javascript
true && true returs true
```|
|```javascript
true && false returns false
```|
|```javascript

```|
|```javascript
false & false returns false
```|
